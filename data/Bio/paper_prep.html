投稿

投稿的经验、教训

  1. 图片上的文字: 矢量化，字体 Arial 或 Times New Roman 选一个坚持到底，字号 6-12points，图的字母编号 14pt。图片大小: (小)9 * 6cm, (中)11 * 11cm, (大)18 * 22cm。
  2. 基因组用 最新 的 GRCh38，fasta 和 gtf 要配套，就是同一家提供的: gencode/NCBI/UCSC选一家用到底。
  3. 基因名字要用标准名字: HGNC: The resource for approved human gene nomenclature.
  4. Please search each and every human gene symbol at www.genenames.org and ensure current and approved nomenclature is used. For mouse symbols, please search www.informatics.jax.org.
  5. Ki67 基因用斜体，蛋白不用斜体。人和鼠的蛋白都要大写。图、表、图例、(附录)也要对应修改。 Proper formatting must also be used; symbols for genes are in italics, and symbols for proteins are in non-italicized font. Both human and mouse proteins should be formatted in all capital letters. Corrections must be reflected in the figures and tables as well as text.
  6. 缩写词要扩展出全称。 PNAS articles should be accessible to a broad scientific audience; as such, please spell out the abbreviation mRNA, cDNA, UTRs, GO, IGV.
  7. 复合词，作形容词时用连字符(single-cell resolution)，作名词时不用连字符。 Certain compound terms are hyphenated when used as adjectives and unhyphenated when used as nouns. This style has been applied consistently throughout where (and if) applicable.
  8. 禁止使用首次、全新(novel targets 的novel)等字样。 Claims of priority or primacy are not allowed, per PNAS policy (https://www.pnas.org/authors/submittingyour- manuscript); therefore, the term 'novel' has been deleted. If you have concerns with this course of action, please reword the sentence or explain why the deleted term should not be considered a priority claim and should be reinstated.
  9. 标出P值，而不是使用星号。使用 1.05 x 10^-15的形式，而不是科学计数法。
