Brain Knowledge Platform (BKP) is an open, online platform for scientific researchers to understand and analyze the mammalian brain in health and disease. The Platform combines the world's most comprehensive brain cell atlas with the software and genetic tools that neuroscience teams need for precision experiments and robust analysis.
